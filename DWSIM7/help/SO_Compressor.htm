Compressor

The compressor is used to provide energy to a vapor stream in the form of pressure. The ideal process is isentropic (constant entropy) and the non-idealities are considered according to the compressor efficiency, which is defined by the user

Input Parameters

Calculation Mode: Either select a fixed OutletPressure, a Delta_P between inlet and outlet or EnergyStream.
Delta P: pressure rise in the compressor (mode Delta_P)
Pressure: pressure at outlet of compressor (mode OutletPressure)
Adiabatic Efficiency: compressor adiabatic efficiency
Ignore liquid in the inlet stream: defines if the calculator should ignore any liquid in the inlet stream

Output Parameters

Delta-T:
temperature increase observed in the compression process.

Power required:
Shaft power required by the compressor. The calculated power is either written into the energy stream (pressure calculation modes) or read from energy stream (energy caclulation mode).
