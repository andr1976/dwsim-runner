PSV Sizing

This utility sizes a Pressure Safety Valve using data from a Valve's inlet Material Stream. Model equations are based on the American Petroleum Institute Standard 520, Part 1 - Sizing, Selection, and Installation of Pressure-relieving Devices.

More information at: http://www.api.org/publications-standards-and-statistics/standards/whatsnew/publication-updates/new-refining-publications/api_std_520p1
