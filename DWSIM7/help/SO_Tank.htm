Tank

In the current version of DWSIM, the Tank works like a fixed pressure drop for the process. This model only works for liquids without a vapour phase.

Input Parameters

Pressure drop: pressure difference between the outlet and inlet streams.
Tank volume: Volume of the fluid to calculate residence time.

Calculation method

The outlet stream pressure is calculated from the inlet pressure and the pressure drop given.
The temperature remains the same. A TP Flash is done to calculate the properties of the outlet stream.
The residence time is calculated from liquid flow and volume of tank.

Output Parameters

Tank fluid residence time: Residence time of fluid.
